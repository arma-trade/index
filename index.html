Project Site Directory

The 'P' Tree is the standard template copied into every new project folder. It is designed to segregate duties between the Technical Office, Site Execution, and Supply Chain while feeding data back to HQ.

Phase 1: Definition

P01 (Scope/Contract) & P02 (Drawings/Schedule)

Phase 2: Execution

P03 (Supply/POs) & P04 (Site Reports/HSE)

Phase 3: Closing

P05 (Invoices) & P06 (As-Built/Handover)

The Project Lifecycle

From the first sales lead to the final handover, data flows through our system in 6 distinct stages.

1

Start & Define

Sales Lead (R01) converts to Signed Contract. Project ID assigned. P01 created.

2

Technical Planning

Tech Office uploads Shop Drawings to P02. Approval is mandatory before purchasing.

3

Procurement

Supply Chain issues POs in P03 based on approved specs. Material delivered to site.

4

Build & Report

Execution begins. Daily Reports & Photos uploaded to P04. Real-time progress tracking.

5

Billing

Finance generates Client Invoices in P05 based on verified P04 progress.

6

Close & Archive

As-Built drawings in P06. TOC signed. Project folder moved to R02 Archive.
